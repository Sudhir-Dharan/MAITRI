Scenario Building: Navigating Plausible Futures

Positive Scenario: Ecosystem Leadership

  • MAITRI becomes the de facto standard in regulated sectors (FinTech, HealthTech).
  • The Agent Marketplace becomes the "GitHub for agentic workflows."
  • Customers see **60-70% cycle time reduction** and **10x feature velocity**.
  • Strategy: Accelerate this future by building our open ecosystem and championing explainability.

Negative Scenario: Fragmentation & Resistance

  • Adoption is slowed by regulatory headwinds and a lack of trust in "black box" AI.
  • Cloud vendors bundle "good enough" basic agents, commoditizing the market.
  • DevSecOps teams resist full autonomy, leading to partial, low-value rollouts.
  • Mitigation: Our explainability-first design, localized compliance, and focus on change management are our primary defenses.
